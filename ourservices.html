Our Services

Cultural Department:

The cultural departments is responsible for providing all the following services:

  • Follow-up with students' registrations at the University of Qatar and Education City universities.

  • Follow-up with the students' educational journey.

  • Communicate with university housing and the students in order to help them solve their educational and their social problems.

  • Give monthly salaries to the students with scholarships.

  • Acts as a link between the ministry of Higher Education and the students and the universities.

  • Ratification of the Diplomas.

  • Establish different cultural events:

    1. Annual meetings in the presence of his highness the Ambassador and the official from the cultural department.

    2. Events for the students to celebrate different occasions such as the National Day for the Sultanate and the Annual Graduation for the university students.

  • Supervise the different cultural activities for the students.

  • Provide the Qatari schools with the necessary heretical tools to help promote the heritage and culture of the Sultanate.

  • Provide the students with the available references to assist them in their educational researches.

    Consular Department:

    The consular departments is responsible for providing all the following services:

  • Endorsements

  • Passports: replacement and production

  • Traffic Passports

  • Visas (for VISA requests and applications please visit: www.rop.gov.om)

  • Financial Stamps

  • Social Security

  • Omani Guests Affairs

  • Citizens' issues

  • Change of the Omani Residence

  • Permits for Burial and/or transportation of the corpse

    Omani Military Attaché in Qatar:

    The Omani Military Attaché in Qatar has appointed Colonel Rashed Ben Hamoud Ben Hamad al-Tamami to be the new attaché of the Omani Military department in Qatar. He started his duty on the 5th of January 2014.

    Vision:

    The Omani Military Attaché in Qatar aims to create mutual collaboration between both the Qatari and Omani Militaries.

    Mission:

    The Omani Military Department supports the military relation and collaboration between the Sultanate of Oman and Qatar. As well as coordinating and managing the exchange of experiences between them.
