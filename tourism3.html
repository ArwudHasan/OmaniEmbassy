Discover Oman ... like never before

Sohar

Al Batinah North Governorate

Home to the western Hajar mountain range and the historically rich cities of Sohar and Saham, the northern Batinah Governorate alongside the southern form the agricultural centre of the Sultanate. Impressive architecture and towering keeps can be spotted throughout the governorate including the Shinas and As Suwayq forts.

Sur

Ash Sharqiyah South Governorate

The southern part of the Ash Sharqiyah Governorate is home to the fishing culture of the eastern coastline of the Sultanate. At Sur, fishermen embark on daily trips and return with fresh catch from the Arabian Sea and parts of the Sea of Oman. Up north the governorate diverges into sandy and agricultural climates making it a colourful mix of ecosystems and landscapes.
