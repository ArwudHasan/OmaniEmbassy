Education in Oman

Sultan Qaboos University:

Sultan Qaboos University is home to 9 different colleges with more than 60 different Bachelor Degrees. The campus portrays the rich heritage of Oman and Islam while incorporating all the efficiencies of modern life.

Set in a valley beneath the stark panorama of the nearby foothills of the Oman Mountains, the University is a tasteful array of buildings designed with arches and courtyards and constructed in white and pink sandstone in a manner that reflects traditional Omani and Islamic architecture. The University was constructed on an axis in the valley with one end aligned toward Mecca.

Sultan Qaboos University is the realization of the promise announced by His. Majesty Sultan Qaboos Bin Said during the 10th anniversary of Oman’s National Day in 1980. Construction started in 1982 and the first Sultan Qaboos University students were enrolled in 1986. In accordance with the Royal Directives of His Majesty, The University commenced with five colleges; Medicine, Engineering, Agriculture, Education and Science. Furthermore the College of Arts was established in 1987, following the College of Commerce and Economics which was developed in 1993. The College of Law joined the University in 2006 and finally the College of Nursing was established in 2008.

For more Information: www.squ.edu.om

University of Nizwa:

The University of Nizwa was established in 2002 by the Decree of Sultan Qaboos as the first non-profit university in the Sultanate of Oman; it remains the only institution of its kind in the nation. It offers more than 20 programs including Diplomas and Bachelor degrees.

The University of Nizwa aspires to be a beacon (minaret) of knowledge and enlightenment as a prominent higher education institution of excellence. It is to achieve a worldwide reputation for the quality of its programs and graduates and international distinction for innovation, progress and development.

As a non-profit academic institution, governed by its faculty, it shall promote positive thinking, preserve the nation’s Islamic and cultural heritage and identity, faith in Allah and loyalty to the country and His Majesty. Its purpose is to broadly educate students and equip them with values, knowledge and life skills needed to enrich their lives and enable them to meaningfully contribute to the progress of society. To achieve its mission, the University shall develop dynamic integrative programs which provide high quality academic training, research and intellectual development.

For more Information: www.unizwa.edu.om

Oman Medical College:

Oman Medical College is a private co-educational college dedicated to the training of medical doctors and Pharmacists. It offers a 7-year program of study leading to the Doctor of Medicine and a four years and a half year program of study leading to the Bachelor of Pharmacy degree.

The curriculum and school leadership are provided by West Virginia University, U.S.A., which give the students an opportunity to obtain a U.S.-style Medical or Pharmacy degree entirely within Oman. The language of instruction is English. The Education Programs offered by the College are approved by the Ministry of Higher Education of the Sultanate of Oman.

Oman Medical College is committed to educating Medical Doctors of the highest quality dedicated to serving the health care needs of the international community and the people of the Sultanate of Oman

The mission of Pharmacy programme in Oman Medical College is to train pharmacists to enable them to serve society as a professional responsible for appropriate use of medications and devices by the patients, to provide public education services, to monitor and manage activities related to medication regimen, to provide services in Pharmaceutical companies in field of quality as well as regulatory control, formulation development, and management of sales.

For more Information: www.omc.edu.om

Middle East College:

Middle East College (MEC) is one of the leading higher education institutions in the Sultanate of Oman with over 5000 students. MEC, which is affiliated to Coventry University, UK, is located in the knowledge Oasis, Muscat.

It is dedicated to educating students and equipping them with the values, Knowledge and life skills needed to enrich their lives and enable them to meaningfully contribute to the progress of society. It provides a creative, nurturing campus environment where our students can make a positive difference in the community, the state and the world.

As it inspires to develop creative minds, Middle East College has for a missions to excel in developing human resources, creating knowledge, and active community engagement for the immediate society & the world at large through enhanced student experience.

For more Information: www.mec.edu.om

German University of Technology in Oman:

German University of Technology in Oman is a private university in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman and was established in 2007 in collaboration with RWTH Aachen University in Germany, one of the leading universities of technology in Europe.

It is dedicated to educating students and equipping them with the values, Knowledge and life skills needed to enrich their lives and enable them to meaningfully contribute to the progress of society. It provides a creative, nurturing campus environment where our students can make a positive difference in the community, the state and the world.

For more Information: www.gutech.edu.om
